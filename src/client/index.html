Vocabmixer

Looking for a fun way to study your vocabulary words?

Mix em!

Put your vocabulary words and their definitions into the mixer, press start, then try to match them back up.

Add the Ingredients

Use this form to add your vocabulary words and the definitions. When you're done, start the mixer.

Adjust the Mix

“Adjust” the mix by moving the words up or down to match the correct definition.
